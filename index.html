The Man Behind the Chair

We've Been Cutting Since Before It Was Cool

SharpCuts was started in 2018 by James Sifuna — a self-taught barber who learned the craft from his uncle in Kisumu and turned it into a full business.

We believe a good haircut isn't just about hair. It's about the 30 minutes you spend in that chair feeling like someone actually cares about how you look when you walk out.

No loud music. No waiting 2 hours. No bad vibes. Just clean cuts and good conversation.
